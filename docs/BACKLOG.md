- **OCR worker — parameterized content-type normalization (M2b #6 follow-up).** The worker dispatch at [api/handlers/media_ingest.py](../api/handlers/media_ingest.py) does `content_type.lower()` + frozenset membership. A non-conforming client sending `image/png; charset=binary` or `image/png;boundary=...` would lower-case correctly but fail allowlist membership and land in `UnsupportedContentType`. Same gap exists pre-PR in the parser branch. Fix: `content_type.split(";")[0].strip().lower()` at the classification site, OR document parameterized MIMEs as out of scope. Browsers don't typically send parameterized image MIMEs in practice; deferred. Surfaced in 2026-05-27 worker-wiring code-CR M2.

At M5 horizontal scale with multiple workers per node, the pool sizing may need explicit tuning. Defer; not a real concern until then. Surfaced in 2026-05-27 worker-wiring code-CR m1.
